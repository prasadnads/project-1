About Me

My name is Sakthiya Nagaselvam.

I am a trained nurse and have been working as a freelance interpreter since 2007. I was sworn in as an interpreter and authorized translator for the Tamil language in 2017 by the Higher Regional Court of Hamm.

My passion for translation began through my work with migrants in a counselling centre at the German Red Cross. As a native tamil, I am not only familiar with this language, but also with the mentality, culture and customs of the people living there. I also see this as one of the most important prerequisites for a successful and trusting cooperation with my customers.
